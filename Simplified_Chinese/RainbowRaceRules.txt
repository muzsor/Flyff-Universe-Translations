#b 竞赛规则 #nb
1. Only players who have applied to enter the
Rainbow Race can participate.

2. The goal of the race is to follow a designated
path and go through Point Circles to obtain
points and finish as fast as possible.

3. During the Rainbow Race, if you go through
the Point Circle that has item boxes, you can
obtain items that can attack others or protect
yourself.
(All items in your inventory are unavailable for
use during the race)

4. Only characters that are online at the start of
the Rainbow Race can participate in the race.
